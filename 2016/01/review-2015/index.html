My 2015

Leave a reply

I thought I might record what happened to me in 2015. Here’s a summary of the summary:

  • Traveled to Atlanta, Chicago (twice), Madison, Kentucky, San Antonio, Colorado Springs, and Cozumel
  • Went to 10 concerts, 2 comedy shows, 1 musical, a science talk, and 5 movies
  • Went to 5 conferences, spoke at 3
  • Went to 2 Cardinals games and 1 minor league baseball game
  • Went camping twice and floating twice

  • Garden Glow at Missouri Botanical Gardens (January 2)
  • School of Rock concert at the Ready Room (January 10)
    • My nephew Jake played in a band that did Rolling Stones covers
    • A band of older kids played Frank Zappa covers
  • Book of Mormon at Peabody Opera House (January 15)
  • Eagle watching in Alton (January 17)
  • Marched in Ferguson for MLK day (January 19)

  • SLUUG tutorial – regular expressions (February 11)

  • STLLUG presentation – How the Web Works (part 2) (March 19)
